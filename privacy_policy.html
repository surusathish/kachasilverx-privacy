KachaSilverX – Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2026

KachaSilverX is a peer-to-peer silver marketplace connecting buyers and sellers of handmade silver ornaments in Salem, Tamil Nadu. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use KachaSilverX:

  • Name – used to identify you on the platform
  • Phone number – used for OTP login and shared with the other party only when a deal is confirmed
  • Email address – used for account linking via Google Sign-In
  • Aadhaar number – collected for identity verification only; only the last 4 digits are retained in masked format (XXXXXXXX-XXXX); the full number is never stored or shared
  • Location (city) – collected to display on your listings and help nearby buyers find you
  • Device token – used to send push notifications about your listings and deals

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To verify your identity and create your account
  • To connect buyers and sellers on the platform
  • To send notifications about deal activity on your listings
  • Your phone number is shared with the other party only after a deal is confirmed between buyer and seller

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your information with third parties except:

  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication and push notifications
  • AWS – for secure cloud hosting of the platform
  • The other party in a confirmed deal – phone number only

4. Data Security

All data is stored securely on AWS RDS (PostgreSQL). Aadhaar numbers are masked before storage (only last 4 digits retained). Communication between the app and our servers is secured. We are actively working towards full HTTPS encryption.

5. Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the app: Profile → Delete Account.

  • If you have no deal history, your account and all data are permanently deleted immediately.
  • If you have deal history, your account is anonymised and your personal details are removed, while transaction records are retained for platform integrity.

6. Children's Privacy

KachaSilverX is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

7. Data Retention

  • Sold listings are archived after 90 days and permanently purged after 365 days.
  • Active listings with no buyer interest are automatically expired after 180 days.
  • Completed deal records (connections) are archived after 180 days once both parties have reviewed.
  • Archived data is permanently deleted after 365 days.

8. Your Rights (DPDP Act 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction – Request correction of inaccurate data (name, location, email can be edited in-app)
  • Erasure – Delete your account and all associated data via Profile → Delete Account
  • Grievance redressal – Contact us at kachasilverx@gmail.com for any data-related concerns

9. Google Play Services & Firebase

KachaSilverX uses Firebase (a Google service) for authentication, push notifications, and analytics. Firebase may collect device identifiers, IP addresses, and app usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
